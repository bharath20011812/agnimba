About Jamal Mohamed College, Trichy

Jamal Mohamed College was founded in 1951, as an affiliated college to the University of Madras and then affiliated to Bharathidasan University, Tiruchirappalli, when it was formed in 1982. Jamal Mohamed College is situated in the central part of Tamil Nadu, Tiruchirappali city.

E- LEARNING

Agni promotes E-learning by the students of all programs. The installed E-learning platform enables the students to get access to all information related to the subjects, and experience 24/7 learning through Participation.

Students’ recruitment by National and Multinational corporate is the top priority at Agni. In order to ensure 100% placement, the training needs of all students are identified, and hands on training are offered by the field experts invited from industry and reputed training companies in India.

INNOVATIVE MANAGEMENT ACTIVITIES

Agni encourages and promotes Student-Centric activities. Students are encouraged to come forward to exhibit their talents in events organizing and indulge in various innovative management activities that mould them in to professional managers.

EXPERIENCED FACULTY
